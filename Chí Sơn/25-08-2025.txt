*Task2: Đã xong ✅ Chuyển về single-node KRaft
    để 1 node vừa làm broker, controller trong môi trường lab,dev còn nếu làm production thì tối thiếu 3 broker, 3 controller
Tạo backup cho file cũ + ghi đè server.properties
    sudo cp /etc/kafka/server.properties /etc/kafka/server.properties.bak
    
    sudo tee /etc/kafka/server.properties >/dev/null <<'EOF'
    process.roles=broker,controller
    node.id=1
    controller.quorum.voters=1@10.0.2.15:9093
    controller.listener.names=CONTROLLER
    listeners=PLAINTEXT://10.0.2.15:9092,CONTROLLER://10.0.2.15:9093
    advertised.listeners=PLAINTEXT://10.0.2.15:9092
    listener.security.protocol.map=CONTROLLER:PLAINTEXT,PLAINTEXT:PLAINTEXT
    inter.broker.listener.name=PLAINTEXT
    log.dirs=/data/apps/kafka/data
    num.partitions=1
    num.recovery.threads.per.data.dir=1
    offsets.topic.replication.factor=1
    transaction.state.log.replication.factor=1
    transaction.state.log.min.isr=1
    share.coordinator.state.topic.replication.factor=1
    share.coordinator.state.topic.min.isr=1
    socket.send.buffer.bytes=102400
    socket.receive.buffer.bytes=102400
    num.network.threads=3
    num.io.threads=8
    socket.request.max.bytes=104857600
    log.retention.hours=168
    log.segment.bytes=1073741824
    log.retention.check.interval.ms=300000
    confluent.license.topic.replication.factor=1
    confluent.metadata.topic.replication.factor=1
    confluent.balancer.topic.replication.factor=1
    confluent.security.event.logger.exporter.kafka.topic.replicas=1
    EOF
Format lại uuid cho server.properties
    kafka-storage format -t E4NV52WlSiiz68OgNLLDDg  -c /etc/kafka/server.properties
Chạy lại confluent
